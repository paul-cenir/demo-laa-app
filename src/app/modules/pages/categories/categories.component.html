Behavioral Manifestation of Group Values

(40%)

In the completion of the achievement the individual or team members must have demonstrated one or more of the following Lopez Values:

PIONEERING ENTREPRENEURAL SPIRIT​

- Envisioning possibilities and having the courage to make them real
- We choose to build businesses that are catalyst for national development

BUSINESS EXCELLENCE

- Being the best in everything we do in order to gain and maintain leadership in the markets we serve
- We conduct our business along a very high bar and hold ourselves accountable for excellence

UNITY

- Having a strong shared identity based on a common vision, mutual respect, "malasakit" and accountability
- We work best when our people and our various businesses collaborate and help each other and constantly build a culture that encourages this

NATIONALISM

- Carrying the name "Filipino" and representing the Philippines with pride
