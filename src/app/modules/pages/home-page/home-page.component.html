The Lopez Achievement Awards, established in 2002, is an annual search for the most exemplary accomplishments of teams and individuals within the Lopez Group of Companies that have contributed significantly to business objectives and at the same time demonstrated the Group's core values. It is granted by the Lopez family, through the Lopez Group Executive Committee.
